System Objective & Architecture

Rising Waters utilizes machine learning algorithms to assess local meteorological indices, historical annual totals, seasonal precipitation trends, and cloud visibilities to classify if conditions pose an immediate danger of a flood event. It serves as a vital component for municipal planners, emergency responders, and environmental scientists to build resilience against extreme climate occurrences.

High Accuracy Predictions

Using calibrated features such as Standard Scaling and outlier treatment, models predict flood thresholds with premium accuracy metrics.

Early Intervention

Real-time predictions allow civil organizations to plan flood response actions hours or days prior to peak rainfall surges.
